Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 4, 2026

Phozi ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your data when you use our services to manage your Google Photos and Google Drive content.

1. Data We Access

Phozi uses Google OAuth2 to access your Google account. We request the following scopes:

  • Google Drive: To list, read, and manage files for backup and organization purposes.
  • Google Photos: To display your gallery, manage albums, and perform duplicate detection.

2. Google User Data - Limited Use Disclosure

Phozi's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. How We Use Data

We do not collect or store your Google data on our servers. All processing (such as duplicate detection and gallery rendering) happens locally on your device. We use your data specifically to:

  • Provide a unified view of your media across multiple accounts.
  • Enable you to upload and organize photos.
  • Identify duplicate files to help you save storage space.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Your authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using encrypted storage.
